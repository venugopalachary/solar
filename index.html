OUR COMPANY

A&T Power Systems, aptly known as ATPOSYS- incepted in 2012 with a mission to create its footprints in green energy domain and contribute to nation building endeavour of a brighter progressive future of coming generations is among the top system integrators of Telangana state and is based out of Hyderabad & Gurgaon (Delhi/NCR).

The Atposys is a green energy power products, services and solutions company with over 2Megawatt root top solar installations covering government institutional and residential entities.

Atposys going by its philosophy of green & sustainable happy life for all has its footprints in both Urban & Rural areas with primary focus on rural India. Some of our most prestigious projects spread across country of which most notable are elite homes in Hyderabad, National Institute of rural Development (NIRD), environmental Protection Training & Research Institute (EPTRI), CSR projects of NTPC, Village electrification of Banjarpalli, Erravalli, Ibrahimpur and tribal Villages of Telangana.

TMoving forward Atposys has ventured into energy audits, smart building solutions , smart green gadgets, consumer durables of reputed brands and also with available senior profile professional resource pool do undertake Business & Brand consulting On the anvil is creative ventures and educational, vocational skill set training ventures
